A Phased Roadmap to Transformation

We recommend an adaptive, multi-phase roadmap that builds capabilities incrementally while delivering continuous value. Foundational work in governance and culture runs parallel to implementation.

Establish governance, launch AI literacy programs, and execute the high-ROI "Autonomous Invoice Processing" pilot. Prove the value and build momentum.

Phase 2: Scale & Expand (Months 6-18)

Deploy 2-3 additional agentic solutions in Sales and HR. Mature governance processes and begin modernizing the legacy CRM system.

Phase 3: Optimize & Transform (Months 18+)

Drive enterprise-wide adoption, establish an AI Center of Excellence (CoE), and begin exploring disruptive AI-driven business models.
